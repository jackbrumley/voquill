Voquill

The original, free, and private system-wide push-to-talk dictation tool.

Zero Cloud

No remote servers, no cloud services, and no data collection. Transcription happens 100% on your device, powered by Whisper.cpp.

Zero Accounts

No login required. No tracking, no onboarding, and no "Free Tiers". You own your software and your voice data.

Zero Cost

Truly free and open source under the MIT license. We believe voice dictation is a utility that should be available to everyone.
